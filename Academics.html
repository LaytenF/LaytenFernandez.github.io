AOIT 2021-2025

AOIT, or the Academy of Informational Technology is a branch of the Academy of Industrial and Engineering
Technology. In AOIT, I learn a vast array of things. We learn how to code, network, and build. This is because
our teacher, Mrs. Cindy Takara realized that not everyone is going to go into the same field. For me, this is
great because I get to learn new things, and decide what I may want to do in the future, like software
engineering. As for leadership, I am the AOIT student represenitive on our board. I am also an ambassador for AOIT.



Highschool Judo 2022-2023

Waipahu also has their own judo program, and I joined it during my sophmore year. I played in multiple
tournaments, being the second seat within Waipahu's weightclasses. I unfortunately was never able to win any
tournaments. However, due to my rapid learning skills, and my leadership skills, I was able to be promoted to the
next rank, blue belt.



NTHS 2023-2025

NTHS, or the National Technical Honor Society is a nationally recognized orginization that focuses and creating
leaders. This is lead by Mrs. Cindy Takara. NTHS is something that challenges me to become better than what I
even know. I am currently going onto my second year within this, and I now am the co-secretary to this orginization.



Esports Club 2021-2024

The Esports club it a club where people come together, and play games, like Super Smash Brothers. The club
originally created by Kellen Inafuku, and was made upon the request of students. However, our club isn't about just
playing games. Recently, we have started to make highschool wide tournaments, and within the club, people push
each other every day to become better. During my time here, I have served as the vice president, and president. I
believe that during my time, I have been able to bring the club to these heights.
